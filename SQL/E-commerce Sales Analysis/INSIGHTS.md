# E-commerce Sales Insights

## 1️⃣ Product Overview
- The store offers products across **four categories**:
  - Electronics
  - Apparel
  - Furniture
  - Footwear
- Product prices range from **$25.00 to $1,200.00**

---

## 2️⃣ Order Analysis
- A total of **4 orders** were recorded in the dataset.
- Orders vary significantly in value, indicating different
  customer spending behaviors.

---

## 3️⃣ Revenue Performance
- The **total revenue generated** from all orders is **$3,115.00**.
- This shows strong revenue contribution
   from a small number of high-value purchases.

---

## 4️⃣ High-Value Products
- The **Laptop** is the most expensive product at **$1,200.00**.
  High-priced products contribute disproportionately to total revenue.

---

## 5️⃣ Highest-Value Order
- The highest-value order was:
  - **Order ID:** 101
  - **Product:** Laptop
  - **Total Price:** $2,400.00
- This single order accounts for a large portion of total revenue.

---

## 6️⃣ Orders Above $200
- **3 out of 4 orders** exceeded $200 in total price.
- This suggests that customers frequently make medium to high-value purchases.

---

## 7️⃣ Business Takeaways
- Premium products drive the majority of revenue.
- Even with a small dataset, **price concentration** is evident.
- Marketing efforts could focus on high-margin products to maximize revenue.
- Product bundles or upselling strategies may increase average order value.
